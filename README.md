# GiftHub
GiftHub is a simple, customizable platform where you can create and share your wishlist with friends and family. Whether itâ€™s for a birthday, holiday, or because household things are expensive and need help when you move to a new place(like me!), GiftHub makes it easy for your loved ones to know exactly what you want, helping them choose the perfect gift.

## Features
- **Personalized Wishlist:** Easily add, edit, and categorize items you wish to receive.
- **Gift Reservation:** Friends and family can reserve items to avoid duplicate gifts.
- **Item Status:** See which items are reserved or already purchased.
- **Secure Sharing:** Share your wishlist via a unique, private link.
